NEWS FROM THE WEST!

BROUGHT TO YOU by yours truly, the man, the myth, the reporter, the legend: the partner who's always lurking at every hot corner of the Frontier sniffing out a hot story. The one and only pistol-packin', part-timin' bounty hunter Abner "Hot-Take" Norman, whose picture lies on this here left wall.

HAVE YOU SEEN THEM?

Don't got no clue who this here man is, but a sheriff once did me a favour and in return I said I'd put this up somewhere.

Davey "Hotpockets" Brutus - toughest man in the west. He so tough we don't got his photo taken (and we ain't got no good sketch drawing man in town neither) - but this here poster shows that sherriff pay a good ammount for his head.

Some damn rummy puncht a hole in this here wall and I won't rest 'till I find this man.

Mary Sue - wanted for stealing my heart in the summer of '68

Mark Twain
